Eugene Tree Coverage Map by Abie Safdie

This map shows urban tree coverage in the City of Eugene.

The city is divided into 1 km² grid blocks. Each block is given a tree score based on the following characteristics:

Scoring Formula:

Score = (0.1 × Total Spread)
      + (0.05 × Total Height)
      + (1 × Total Trees)
      + (450 × (Total Parks ≤ 1))
      + (25 × (Total Parks > 1))
      + (10 × Total Species)
(Note: Score is then normalized 
on a 0-100 scale)

Data Source: https://mapping.eugene-or.gov/
